COACH JOHN

USAT LEVEL 1 Certified Coach

John raced his first triathlon at six years old and was hooked on the sport ever since. He competed in triathlons every summer throughout middle school, high school, and college.

John was a high school swimmer and a collegiate runner for RPI cross country and track. He graduated with a master's degree in Electrical Engineering from RPI.

John earned his professional triathlete license in 2022. He has raced for the USA in several draft legal continental cups and half ironmans at the professional level.

John has always had a desire to coach and share his knowledge with athletes looking to maximize their performance.

John's engineering mindset combined with his background as a collegiate and professional athlete and three years of coaching experience provides his athletes with all the tools for success in endurance sport.
